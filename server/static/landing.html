How it works

1

Start the bot

Open @GiveWeiBot in Telegram. Your HD wallet is derived automatically — no seed phrase to manage.

2

Fund your wallet

Use /address to get your deposit address, then send USDC on Avalanche or Base. Check your balance anytime with /balance.

3

Top up any address

Run /topup 0xABC... 5 ETH.ETH to swap $5 of USDC into ETH and send it to any address. Track progress with /status.
