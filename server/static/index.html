Three steps. That's it.

Deposit USDC, pick a destination, and the bot handles the rest.

1

Get your address

Send /address to get your deposit wallet. Each user (or group chat) gets a unique address derived automatically.

2

Deposit USDC

Send USDC to your address on Base or Avalanche. These are the only two source chains — the bot picks the best one at swap time. Check anytime with /balance.

3

Top up anything

Run /topup 0xABC... 5 ETH.ETH to swap $5 USDC into ETH and deliver it anywhere. Add a routing hint to choose your provider.

Three providers, one command

The bot queries all providers and picks the best price — or you can force a specific one with a routing hint.

Thorchain

DEX · Non-custodial

Cross-chain liquidity protocol. Swaps happen on-chain — your USDC goes directly from your wallet to the protocol. No intermediaries.

SimpleSwap

Private · Custodial

Exchange aggregator supporting 29+ assets. Custodial — USDC is sent to a deposit address and SimpleSwap delivers the output to your destination.

Near Intents

DEX · Intent-based

Intent-based cross-chain swaps via the NEAR protocol. USDC is deposited to a one-time address and solvers compete to fill your order at the best rate.
