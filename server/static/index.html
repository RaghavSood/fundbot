Instant Top-ups

Fund any address in one command. GiveWei finds the best route, executes the swap from USDC, and delivers tokens in under a minute.

Telegram Native

No accounts, no passwords, no browser extensions. Open the bot in Telegram and start funding — in DMs or group chats.

Self-Hosted

Team Wallets

Every swap is tracked in real-time. Get notified the moment your top-up completes, or check status anytime.

Multi-Chain

Three commands. That's it.

No setup wizards, no configuration files. Just start chatting.

1

Get your address

Send /address to get your unique deposit address. Your wallet is derived automatically — no seed phrase to manage.

2

Deposit USDC

Send USDC on Avalanche or Base to your address. Check your balance anytime with /balance.

3

Top up anything

Run /topup 0xABC... 5 ETH.ETH to swap $5 of USDC into ETH and deliver it to any address. Done.
